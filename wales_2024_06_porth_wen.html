A short hike to some seaside industrial ruins.

As part of our week at Maes Yr Hendre holiday cottage we had a trip to the Porth Wen Brickworks. A derelict, abandoned Victorian brickworks located on the north coast of Anglesey at Porth Wen (White Bay).

Parking by the side of the road here, this is the very simple route we took to the coast and the ruins.



Here's Tiggy taking a break by the road whilst we went off for a walk and a picnic.



The path starts off level and grassy, its quite easy for the whole route but does get a little steeper.



The view of Porth Wen.



The path getting gravelier and steeper down to the ruins



And a lot of pictures of the ruined buildings, machinery, and beehive kilns.



















As we headed back, one last look back at the ruins and Porth Wen.

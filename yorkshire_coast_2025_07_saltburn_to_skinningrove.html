Saltburn to Skinningrove
Date:
10 July 2025
Distance:
7.5 miles / 12 km
Time:
3 hours 30 mins

A long evening walk along the cliffs to Skinningrove.

Park for free by the woods here, the first path into the woods is part of the Cleveland Way. Then meander through the woods downhill, following the route below to get to Skelton beck, then follow the beck north to the beach.

Chop gate to the Bride Stones
Date:
21 July 2025
Distance:
5.6 miles/9 km
Time:
3 hours

Parking at Chop Gate and heading East uphill to Tripsdale Beck then along the top of the moors to the Bride Stones.

Most importantly, the second part of this route was a nightmare. Definitely not a route I will be doing again! Heading down from the moors and into the farm fields was a very tricky path and one not many people had used for a long time. Very overgrown with bracken and lots of marshy bits, nettles and thistles.

Parking at the usual spot in Chop Gate at the Village Hall car park.. Heading East through some fields and a farm up to the moors and the old picnic spot at Tripsdale Beck. Then back to the top of the hill and along the track south to the Bride Stones. After that it was all downhill, literally and figuratively, along difficult to find paths and through allsorts of overgrown vegetation along the valley bottom back to Chop Gate.



Uphill through the farm, continue uphill. Note the path on the right which we';ll be travelling back along.

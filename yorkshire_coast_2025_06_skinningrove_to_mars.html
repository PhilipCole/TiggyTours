Skinningrove to Mars
Date:
29 January 2025
Distance:
2 miles / 3 km
Time:
1 hour 30 mins

A short walk through the Saltburn valley gardens, woods and to the pier.

Park for free by the seaside here, the first path into the woods is part of the Cleveland Way. Then meander through the woods downhill, following the route below to get to Skelton beck, then follow the beck north to the beach.

Date:
21 June 2024
Distance:
2 miles / 3 km
Time:
1 hour

A stroll along a river

One of the adventures we took part in (see other links below) as part of the North Wales Tiggy Tour, parking at Cae Llan Pay & Display Car Park and walking along a river bank.

The route starts in Betws-Y-Coed following a path parallel to the river. There is a longer river route but after a mile we turned back, following a path into some woods and then onto a road back to Betws-Y-Coed.



Here are some pics I took on the Betws-Y-Coed bridge.









A blurry pic as I was in a hurry, Ruby was getting impatient with all the photo taking. This pic shows some of the path was a wooden walkway in places.



Most of the path was dirt, with a few tree roots thrown in.









After a mile along the river bank it was time to turn back. Instead of following the same path back we took an alternate route through the woods.





Once through the woods you join a local, quiet, road back to Betws-Y-Coed.

The Himalayan Garden & Sculpture Park
Date:

A day trip out to see gardens, ponds and sculptures.

Set in the beautiful countryside between Masham and Ripon in North Yorkshire, the award-winning The Himalayan Garden & Sculpture Park is home to 90 striking contemporary sculptures, situated within 45 acres of stunning woodland and gardens.

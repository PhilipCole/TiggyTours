Keld Waterfalls
Date:
19 August 2022
Distance:
2.7 miles / 4.3 km
Time:
1 hour 30 mins

Four Waterfalls in one hike.

Parking here in Keld the route takes in East Gill Force at East Stoneside, Kisdon Force, Currack Force and Wain Wath Force.



Leaving Keld in a South Easterly direction and following a path to Birk Hill we got to see the first waterfall on our list. Kisdon Force.







Above the waterfall things are a lot calmer.





Looking down from the top of Kisdon Force.



Then it was a case of heading back the way we came, then crossing the river and heading North.



There are some well established paths between the waterfalls.



The second waterfall, East Gill Force.



Then heading West, parallel to the river with some great views that don't involve water.





And a sketchy bridge.



The third waterfall, Currack Force.



After Currack Force it was a short footpath to the road, crossing a roadbridge for a nice view up the river.



Turning right and following the B6270, a very quiet country road, to the fourth and final waterfall, Wain Wath Force.



We then headed back dow the road to Keld and Tiggy.

Thirlmere Infinity Pool and Reservoir

A long drive for a short hike. I'd seen the infinity pool a couple of times online so made a note of the location and planned a route on the OS Maps website.

There are a couple of car parks by this part of Thirlmere but I chose this long section of layby here to leave Tiggy at.

The route starts at the side of the road, following it South for a quick trip over the road to see Thirlmere Reservoir. Then through Swirls Carpark and North, steadily uphill to the plunge pool. After a plunge the route follows the waterfalls down to the main path where you loop back to where you'd been earlier with a different way back to the layby via Thirlspot Farm.



A nice view for Tiggy as I left her in a layby.



Follow the road, this pic is looking back at the layby.



Crossing the road for a slight detour to checkout Thirlmere close up.









Then head back to the road and pass through Swirls Carpark. There are a couple of streams and footbridges to cross as you head North on a footpath to the waterfalls.







With small waterfalls and great views across the valley.





Follow the signpost to Stanah Sticks Pass, but don't forget this signpost, we'll be heading back to it to travel back to Tiggy via Thirlspot Farm.



Some more views across the valley.





Then after a short uphill trek you get to the inifity pool here. There is a bit of a steep but short scramble down to it, but it is a well worn path.



As you can see form the above pic, it was a little busy and as there was a queue I decided to follow the stream just a little bit upstream to find a smaller, antisocial plunge pool.





After a relaxing plunge I headed down the path parallel to the waterfalls and Fisherplace Gill. Here you can see the path to the right of the Gill and the various waterfalls.



This is a view of the infinity pool itself and the waterfall below it. As I found, there are other pools along the Gill.







Head down alongside the Gill until you get to a footbridge and turn left, heading South and back to the signpost we saw earlier, at this junction.



This time heading down the hill to Thirlspot Farm.



One final look back at where I'd headed from, the path from the infinity pool followed the wall in this pic.



Thirlspot Farm is right by the main road, turn left and follow the road back to the layby.

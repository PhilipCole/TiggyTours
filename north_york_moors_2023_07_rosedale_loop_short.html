Rosedale Ana cross small loop
Date:
1 July 2023
Distance:
2.8 Miles/4.2 KM
Time:
1 hour 30 mins

The smaller loop, due to bracken.

This walk was a bit more of a reconoiter as I planned parking from a spot I'd seen during the Blakey Ridge loop hike. I'd planned a bit more of a hike on the moors but the path up was wet and overgrown so I shortened it and planned another route for another time.

The shorter route was clockwise, following the road into Rosedale Abbey before heading right along a track. After following the track for a while it was timne to head uphill, firstly through fields but then through bracken and heather where it got tough. After getting to the top of the hill following the track over to Ana Cross and then heading back to the road.

The Holy Island.

A day trip to the Holy Island with some Mead testing thrown in. Note that the island is only accessible at low tide so use Holy Island Safe Crossing Times to check you can get there...and back!

After driving up the northumberland coast turn right across the causeway.



Lots of parking available and then its time to wander on foot to the castle itself.



Also on the island is Lindisfarne Priory.
